How to play

Each round one auction card is revealed. Everyone bids in secret; the highest bid wins. Treasures give you gems for your collection. Loans give you cash now but cost you at scoring. Invests are free money. The winner reveals one gem from their hand into the public Value Display, which sets how much each color of gem is worth at end-of-game (per the chosen chart). Missions auto-complete when your collection covers them.
